1. Core Responsibilities of a Product Manager

A Product Manager (PM) is responsible for what gets built and why.

They are the voice of the customer, business, and market.

Key Functions

1. Product Vision

Define long-term product direction

Ensure alignment with business strategy

Translate vision into roadmap

2. Customer & Market Understanding

Identify customer needs

Conduct competitive analysis

Validate product-market fit

3. Roadmap Ownership

Prioritize features

Sequence product development

Balance business value vs engineering effort

4. Requirements Definition

Define product requirements

Create PRDs and user stories

Clarify acceptance criteria

5. Cross Functional Alignment
   Coordinate with:

Engineering

Marketing

Sales

Customer Success

Leadership

6. Product Success Metrics
   Define and track:

Adoption

Retention

Revenue

Usage

Performance

2. Core Responsibilities of a Project Manager

A Project Manager (PgM) is responsible for how work gets executed.

They ensure delivery happens predictably and efficiently.

Key Functions

1. Project Planning

Define scope

Establish timelines

Break work into deliverables

2. Task Coordination

Track workstreams

Assign responsibilities

Remove blockers

3. Timeline Governance

Milestone tracking

Critical path management

Delivery forecasting

4. Risk Management

Identify risks early

Mitigate issues

Escalate when needed

5. Communication
   Maintain constant communication with:

Product

Engineering

Marketing

Leadership

6. Delivery Accountability
   Ensure:

deadlines are met

dependencies resolved

teams stay aligned

They are the control tower of the product system.
