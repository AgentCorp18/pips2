The PIPS Framework

Six steps. Every problem. Built into your workflow.

A proven methodology that guides your team from problem identification through root cause analysis to measurable results.

1

Identify

Define the problem with precision. Who is affected? What does success look like? A well-defined problem is half-solved.

2

Analyze

Dig into root causes using fishbone diagrams, 5 Whys, and Pareto analysis. Stop treating symptoms.

3

Generate

Brainstorm solutions with your team. Capture every idea. Use structured ideation tools to go beyond the obvious.

4

Select & Plan

Evaluate options with decision matrices and cost-benefit analysis. Build a plan with clear owners, timelines, and milestones.

5

Implement

Execute the plan with integrated ticketing, task management, and progress tracking. The work happens here.

6

Evaluate

Measure results against your original problem statement. Capture lessons learned. Feed insights back to Step 1.
